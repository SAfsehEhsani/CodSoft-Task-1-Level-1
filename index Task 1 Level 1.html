Introduction

Dinosaurs were a group of reptiles that lived on Earth for over 245 million years and were the dominant land animals for more than 100 million years.Dinosaurs came in many shapes and sizes, from the giant Spinosaurus to the chicken-sized Microraptor, and were able to survive in a variety of ecosystems. They were ancestrally bipedal, but many extinct groups included quadrupedal species, and some were able to shift between these stances. Dinosaurs became extinct about 65.5 million years ago.
